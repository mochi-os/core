## Hlonipha

- Akukho kuxhatshazwa, kungcungcuthekiswa, kusongelwa, okanye ukulandelwa okuqhubekayo komntu okanye iqela.
- Akukho ntetho yenzondo: umxholo ohlasela okanye ozenza abantu bangabi nabuntu ngenxa yobuhlanga, imvelaphi yesizwe, unqulo, ubuhlanga, isini, ubume besini, ukuba sondelelene ngokwesondo, ukukhubazeka, okanye ukugula okuqatsele awuvunyelwa.
- Akukho ukuxhokonxa ubundlobongela kubantu okanye kwiimpahla.
- Ukungavumelani kulungile; ukuxhatshazwa akulunganga. Gxeka iimbono, hayi isidima somntu osivumayo.

## Musa ukunika i-spam okanye ukukhohlisa

- Akukho mxholo wezohrhwebo ongacelwanga ngobuninzi, i-spam yokulandela/yokuphawula, okanye ukuziphatha okungekho mthethweni okuququzelelweyo.
- Akukho ukuzenza abanye abantu, imibutho, okanye iindima ngendlela enenjongo yokukhohlisa. Ukulinganisa neeakhawunti zabalandeli ezicacileyo zinelebhile kulungile.
- Akukho i-phishing, ukusasazwa kwe-malware, okanye iikhonkco zokuvuna iziqinisekiso.
- Akukho ukutshintshwa ngokungemvelo kweevoti, ukubekwa kwesinyathelo, okanye imilinganiselo yotyhubeko.

- Musa ukurekhoda okanye wabelane ngeengxoxo zabucala phakathi kwabantu ababini ngaphandle kwemvume yelinye iqela, apho imvume ifunwa ngumthetho.
