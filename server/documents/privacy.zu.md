## Yini esiyiqoqayo

Lapho ubhalisa futhi usebenzise le seva siqoqa:

- **Idatha ye-akhawunti**: igama lomsebenzisi, izindlela zokuqinisekisa ozikhethile (ikheli le-imeyili, ama-passkey, i-app yokuqinisekisa, amakhodi okutakula, noma i-akhawunti evela kumhlinzeki wokungena weqembu lesithathu), nanoma yiluphi ulwazi lwephrofayili olungezayo (igama elibonisiwe, isithombe somuntu, ibhayografi).
- **Okuqukethwe okudalayo**: ukuthumela, imiyalezo, amazwana, amafayela, nanoma yiyiphi enye idatha okhetha ukuyishicilela ngezinhlelo zokusebenza ezifakwe kule seva.
- **Idatha yobuchwepheshe**: ikheli le-IP, ulwazi lwesiphequluli/idivayisi, nezitembu zezikhathi zezicelo ozenzayo. Lokhu kusetshenziselwa ukugcina insiza iphephile futhi isebenza, futhi ukuze kutholakale ukuxhashazwa.
- **Amakhukhi**: ikhukhi lesikhathi sokungena lisethwa lapho ungena. Ligcina kuphela isikhombi sesikhathi sokungena esingaqondiwe; asisebenzisi amakhukhi ukukhangisa noma ukulandelela kumasayithi ahlukene.

Asidayisi idatha yakho yomuntu siqu, futhi asabelane ngayo nabakhangisi.

## Indlela i-Mochi ephatha ngayo idatha yakho (lokhu kubalulekile)

I-Mochi yi-**inethiwekhi yontanga (P2P)**, hhayi isikhungo seseva eyodwa. Lokho kushintsha ukuthi idatha yakho ihamba kanjani ngezindlela ezibalulekile zobumfihlo bakho:

- Okuqukethwe okushicilela kubalandeli, ababhalisi, noma ezindaweni ezabiwayo **kuyakhushulwa kwamanye amaseva e-Mochi** asingethe labo balandeli noma ababhalisi. {{operator.name}} ngeke akwazi ukususa amakhophi okuqukethwe kwakho kumaseva angawasebenzisi. Uma ususa okuthunyelwe, le seva izothumela isaziso sokususa kontanga eyithumele kubo ngaphambili, kodwa ukususa kungumzamo omuhle futhi kuxhomeke ekubambisaneni kontanga laba.
- **Ubunikazi bakho (igama lomsebenzisi, iphrofayili, amakhi omphakathi)** kushicilelwa kuhla lwemibhalo lwe-Mochi uma ukhetha iphrofayili eyomphakathi, ukuze abanye abasebenzisi be-Mochi bakwazi ukukuthola futhi baxhumane nawe.
- Okuqukethwe okuqondile, okuyimfihlo (ama-draft, amanothi ayimfihlo, imiyalezo eyimfihlo phakathi kwabamukeli abathile) kuhlala kule seva nakumaseva abamukeli kuphela; akusiyo ingxenye yenethiwekhi ka-P2P yomphakathi.

Mfushane: noma yini okhetha ukuyabelana nabanye abantu ku-Mochi izohamba ngaphandle kokulawula kuka-{{operator.name}}, ngokwakha. Phatha okuqukethwe okushicilela njengoba ungenza nge-imeyili noma kunoma yikuphi ukuxhumana okuhlanganisiwe.
