# Mochi server rules

These rules apply to everyone using this Mochi server, operated by **{{operator.name}}**, regardless of which domain or URL is used to reach it. They apply to all content you post and all interactions with other users on this server, including content that is replicated from this server to other peers on the Mochi network.

By using this server you agree to follow these rules. Breaches may result in content removal, account suspension, or account closure under the **Terms and conditions**.

## Be respectful

- No harassment, bullying, threats, or sustained targeting of any individual or group.
- No hate speech: content that attacks or dehumanises people based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, caste, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, or serious illness is not allowed.
- No incitement to violence against people or property.
- Disagreement is fine; abuse is not. Critique ideas, not the dignity of the person holding them.

## Stay legal

- No content that is illegal under the law of **{{operator.jurisdiction}}**.
- No child sexual abuse material. There are no exceptions, and accounts that post or attempt to obtain such material will be reported to the appropriate authorities.
- No content that incites or facilitates terrorism, organised criminal violence, or human trafficking.
- No content that glorifies or incites cruelty to animals. Reporting and journalism that exposes such cruelty in order to condemn it is fine.
- No content that infringes copyright, trademark, or other intellectual-property rights of others. If you receive a takedown request and you believe it is in error, contact us.

## Don't spam or deceive

- No bulk unsolicited commercial messaging, follow/comment spam, or coordinated inauthentic behaviour.
- No impersonation of other people, organisations, or roles in a way intended to mislead. Parody and clearly-labelled fan accounts are fine.
- No phishing, malware distribution, or credential-harvesting links.
- No artificial manipulation of votes, rankings, or engagement metrics.

## Respect privacy

- Do not post other people's private personal data (home address, phone number, government identifiers, private messages) without their consent.
- Do not post nude or pornographic content of real people without their consent. This applies equally to original photos or video, and to manipulated or AI-generated images that depict identifiable real people.
- Do not record or share private one-to-one conversations without the other party's consent, where consent is required by law.

## Adult content

The default policy on this server is that **adult/sexual content is allowed only if** clearly marked as sensitive, hidden behind a content warning, and not featuring minors or non-consenting subjects. {{operator.name}} may set a stricter policy on this server. When in doubt, ask.

## Reporting violations

If you encounter content or behaviour that breaches these rules, please report it. Many apps include a "Report" option on individual posts and profiles. For anything that requires direct attention from {{operator.name}}, including legal complaints and emergencies, email **{{operator.email}}**

## Enforcement

{{operator.name}} enforces these rules at its discretion, taking into account context, intent, severity, and any prior history. We aim to be consistent and proportionate. We may remove specific content, hide content behind a warning, suspend an account, or close an account permanently. Decisions are final, but you may write to **{{operator.email}}** to appeal. We read every appeal.

## Changes

We may update these rules from time to time. Material changes will be announced on the server or the Mochi Announcements feed before they take effect.
