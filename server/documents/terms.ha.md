## Amfani da ake yarda

Amfani da wannan sabar yana ƙarƙashin **Ƙa'idojin sabar**. Duk abin da ya keta ƙa'idoji - gami da abun ciki ba bisa doka ba, cin zarafi, spam, yin kama, da cin zarafin sauran masu amfani - an haramta shi.

Ta hanyar buga abun ciki ta wannan sabar kana ba {{operator.name}} da fadi cibiyar Mochi lasisi mai banda-keɓancewa, na duniya, ba tare da kuɗi ba don host, adana, replicate, watsa, nunawa, da cache abun cikin **kamar yadda ake buƙata don gudanar da sabis ɗin** - gami da P2P replication da aka bayyana a sanarwarmu ta **Privacy**. Wannan lasisi yana ƙarewa lokacin da ka share abun cikin, sai dai kwafi da aka riga aka replicate zuwa peers za su ci gaba a wajen ikonmu har sai waɗannan peers sun aiwatar da sharewa.

## Babu garanti

An ba da sabis ɗin **"kamar yadda yake"** da **"kamar yadda yake samuwa"**, ba tare da garanti na kowane iri ba, na bayyane ko na alama. Ba mu garanti cewa sabis ɗin zai kasance ba tare da katsewa ba, ba tare da kuskure ba, mai aminci, ko zai biya takamaiman buƙatunka. Isar da P2P shine mafi-ƙoƙari: ba za mu iya tabbatar da cewa kowane takamaiman saƙo zai kai ga kowane takamaiman peer a kowane takamaiman lokaci ba.
